About Me

I am Xaidie Campbell. I have been an English as a Second Language Teacher for the last 2 decades. My approach is vocabulary first, pronunciation second, and grammar, while important, third. It is based on helping you speak and understand English.

I started my own ESL brand with my YouTube channel: English in TheXarden. I teach the most common 3000 words in English, one word per day. I also have a series of shorts about phrasal verbs, because those are the hardest for non-native English speakers to understand, but native speakers use them more frequently than their synonyms. My goal is to make native English accessible to non-native speakers.
